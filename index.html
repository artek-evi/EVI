About EVI

What's EVI?

EVI is a management platform that, through 3D modeling and rendering, allows visitors to explore the space, creating an immersive and interactive experience. It’s an effective tool that delivers realistic results, even from a distance.
Users can walk through, interact with the project, receive information, see and make presentations, videoconferences and much more with just one click In addition to a high level of connectivity we apply the design as an enhancer, creating an engaging environment.

What does EVI include?

EVI is a self managing platform, where clients can load all the information they want onto the virtual space, like configuration of the necessary links for the project: products, video and file downloads.
EVI offers 3D modeling and rendering specially designed by Artek according to client corporate identity and needs. It allows adding interactions and embedded information.
You will find a project dashboard with metrics visualization and the following indicators: logins, project clicks, flow per day, and more.

How do I navigate?

The user can walk through and interact with the project using a computer, tablet, phone or virtual reality device obtaining all the information needed.
ARTEK will advise you on what type of device is recommended for your project depending on the format or your marketing strategy.
Virtual reality can be used for different types of industries and markets. Every client has a different target and it is important for us to know what you are looking to achieve so we can customize your project in order to hit your goals!
